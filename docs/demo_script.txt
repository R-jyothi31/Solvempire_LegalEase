DEMO SCRIPT – LEGALEASE

1. INTRODUCTION
Hello everyone, we are Team 2 and our project is LegalEase, an AI Legal Document Assistant.
This project helps users understand legal documents such as rental agreements, employment contracts, consumer complaints, and legal notices in simple language.

2. PROBLEM
Most users do not understand legal language in agreements and notices.
They may sign documents without knowing risky clauses or their legal rights.
Our project solves this by using RAG and AI agents.

3. PROJECT FLOW
First, the user uploads a legal PDF.
The system extracts text from the PDF.
Then the document parser agent identifies the document type and extracts important clauses.
The rights agent retrieves relevant legal rights from the legal knowledge base.
The explainer agent converts legal language into simple English.
The risk agent detects risky or unfair clauses.
The next steps agent suggests what the user should do next.

4. DEMO
Now we will upload a legal PDF.
After upload, the system identifies the document type.
It displays extracted clauses.
It shows relevant laws, simple explanation, risks, and suggested next steps.
The FAQ section also allows the user to ask questions about the uploaded document.

5. CONCLUSION
LegalEase helps common people understand legal documents easily and supports better legal awareness using AI.
Thank you.
