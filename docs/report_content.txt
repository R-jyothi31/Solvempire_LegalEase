PROJECT TITLE:
LegalEase – AI Legal Document Assistant

TEAM MEMBERS:
1. Ravipati Jyothi
2. Sri Vasavi Boddu
3. Lakshmi Lavanya Kothalanka

PROJECT ABSTRACT:
LegalEase is an AI-powered legal document assistant that helps users understand legal documents such as consumer complaints, rental agreements, employment contracts, and legal notices in simple language. The system uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and multiple AI agents to extract clauses, retrieve legal rights, explain legal content, detect risks, and suggest next steps.

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Many people sign agreements, receive notices, or handle consumer complaints without understanding legal language. LegalEase solves this by simplifying legal content into understandable English/Telugu and guiding users with legal rights and suggested actions.

OBJECTIVES:
1. Upload and analyze legal PDF documents
2. Detect document type automatically
3. Extract clauses from the document
4. Retrieve relevant laws and legal rights
5. Explain clauses in simple language
6. Flag risky clauses
7. Suggest next steps
8. Support FAQ mode for user questions

TECHNOLOGIES USED:
- Python
- Streamlit
- LangChain
- ChromaDB / FAISS
- HuggingFace Embeddings
- PyMuPDF
- OpenAI / Gemini API

MODULES:
1. PDF Loader
2. Chunking and Embedding Module
3. Document Parser Agent
4. Rights Agent
5. Explainer Agent
6. Risk Agent
7. Next Steps Agent
8. FAQ Agent
9. Streamlit Frontend

WORKFLOW:
1. User uploads PDF
2. Text extracted from PDF
3. Document type identified
4. Clauses extracted
5. Legal rights retrieved
6. Clauses simplified
7. Risks flagged
8. Next steps generated
9. Output shown in frontend

RESULT:
LegalEase can analyze uploaded legal documents, identify clauses, explain them in simple language, retrieve related rights, and suggest what users should do next.

FUTURE SCOPE:
