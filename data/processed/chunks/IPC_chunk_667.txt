Section 509. Word, gesture or act intended to insult the modesty of a woman
Whoever, intending to insult the modesty of any woman, utters any word, 
makes any sound or gesture, or exhibits any object, intending that such word 
or sound shall be heard, of that such gesture or object shall be seen, by such 
woman, or intrudes upon the privacy of such woman, 1[shall be punished with 
simple imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years, and also 
with fine.]
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Simple imprisonment for 1 year, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the woman whom it 
was intended to insult or whose privacy was intruded upon with the 
permission of the court.
1. Inserted by Section 509 of ‘The Criminal Law (Amendment) Act, 2013′
