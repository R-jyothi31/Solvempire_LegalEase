Section 251. Delivery of Indian coin, possessed with knowledge that it is altered
Whoever, having coin in his possession with respect to which the offence 
defined in section 247 or 249 has been committed, and having known at the 
time when he became possessed of such coin that such offence had been 
committed, delivers such coin to any other person, or attempts to induce any 
other person to receive the same, shall be punished with imprisonment of 
either description for a term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be 
liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 10 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable
—Triable by Court of Session—Non-compoundable.
