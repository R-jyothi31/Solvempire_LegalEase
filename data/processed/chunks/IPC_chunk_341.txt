Section 282. Conveying person by water for hire in unsafe or overloaded vessel
Whoever knowingly or negligently conveys, or causes to be conveyed for hire, 
any person by water in any vessel, when that vessel is in such a state or so 
loaded as to endanger the life of that person , shall be punished with 
imprisonment or either description for a term which may extend to six 
months, or with fine which may extend to one thousand rupees, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 6 months, or fine of 1,000 rupees, or both—
Cognizable—Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
