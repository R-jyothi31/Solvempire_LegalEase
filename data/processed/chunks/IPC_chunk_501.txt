[Vide Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1993, sec. 2 (w.e.f. 13-7-1993)].Section 381. Theft by clerk or servant of 
property in possession of master
Whoever, being a clerk or servant, or being employed in the capacity of a clerk 
or servant, commits theft in respect of any property in the possession of his 
master or employer, shall be punished with imprisonment of either 
description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be 
liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—
