Section 124A. Sedition
1124A. Sedition.— Whoever, by words, either spoken or written, or by signs, or 
by visible representation, or otherwise, brings or attempts to bring into hatred 
or contempt, or excites or attempts to excite disaffection towards. 2[* * *] the 
Government established by law in 3[India], 4[* * *] shall be punished 
with 5[imprisonment for life], to which fine may be added, or with 
imprisonment which may extend to three years, to which fine may be added, 
or with fine.
