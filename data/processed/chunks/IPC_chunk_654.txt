Section 500. Punishment for defamation
Whoever defames another shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a 
term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Para I
Punishment—Simple imprisonment for 2 years, or fine, or both—Non-
cognizable—Bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Compoundable by the 
person defamed.
Para II
Punishment—Simple imprisonment for 2 years, or fine, or both—Non-
cognizable—Bailable—Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Compoundable 
by the person defamed with the permission of the court.
