CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Para I
Punishment—Imprisonment for life, or imprisonment for 14 years, with or 
without fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-
compoundable.
Para II
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years, with or without fine—Cognizable—
Non-bailable—Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
Para III
Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
