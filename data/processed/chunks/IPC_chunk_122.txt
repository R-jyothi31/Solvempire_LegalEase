Section 120B. Punishment of criminal conspiracy
1120B. Punishment of criminal conspiracy.—(1) Whoever is a party to a 
criminal conspiracy to commit an offence punishable with 
death, 2[imprisonment for life] or rigorous imprisonment for a term of two 
years or upwards, shall, where no express provision is made in this Code for 
the punishment of such a conspiracy, be punished in the same manner as if he 
had abetted such offence.
