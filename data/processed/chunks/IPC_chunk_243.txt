Section 205. False personation for purpose of act or proceeding in suit or prosecution
Whoever falsely personates another, and in such assumed character makes 
any admission or statement, or confesses judgment, or causes any process to 
be issued or becomes bail or security, or does any other act in any suit or 
criminal prosecution, shall be punished with imprisonment of either 
description for a term which may extend to three years or with fine, or with 
both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 years, or fine, or both—Non-cognizable—
