Section 294A. Keeping lottery office
1[294A. Keeping lottery office.—Whoever keeps any office or place for the 
purpose of drawing any lottery 2[not being 3[a State lottery] or a lottery 
authorised by the 4[State] Government], shall be punished with imprisonment 
of either description for a term which may extend to six months, or with fine, 
or with both.
And whoever publishes any proposal to pay any sum, or to deliver any goods, 
or to do or forbear doing anything for the benefit of any person, on any event 
or contingency relative or applicable to the drawing of any ticket, lot, number 
or figure in any such lottery, shall be punished with fine which may extend to 
one thousand rupees.]
