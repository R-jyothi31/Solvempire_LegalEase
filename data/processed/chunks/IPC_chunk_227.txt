Explanation 3
An investigation directed by a Court of Justice, according to law, and 
conducted under the authority of a Court of Justice, is a stage of a judicial 
proceeding, though that investigation may not take place before a Court of 
Justice.
Illustration
A, in any enquiry before an officer deputed by a Court of Justice to ascertain 
on the spot the boundaries of land, makes on oath a statement which he knows
to be false. As this enquiry is a stage of a judicial proceeding. A has given false 
evidence.
