Section 65. Limit to imprisonment for non-payment of fine, when imprisonment and fine
awardable
The term for which the Court directs the offender to be imprisoned in default 
of payment of a fine shall not exceed one-fourth of the term of imprisonment 
which is the maximum fixed for the offence, if the offence be punishable with 
imprisonment as well as fine.
Section 66. Description of imprisonment for non-payment of fine
The imprisonment which the Court imposes in default of payment of a fine 
may be of any description to which the offender might have been sentenced 
for the offence.
Section 67. Imprisonment for non-payment of fine when offence punishable with fine 
only
