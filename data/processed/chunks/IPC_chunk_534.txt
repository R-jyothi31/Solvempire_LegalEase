Whoever, being a clerk or servant or employed as a clerk or servant, and being 
in any manner entrusted in such capacity with property, or with any dominion
over property, commits criminal breach of trust in respect of that property, 
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which 
may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—
Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Compounded by the owner of the 
property in respect of which the breach of trust has been committed with the 
permission of the court.
