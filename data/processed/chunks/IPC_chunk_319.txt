Section 260. Using as genuine a Government stamp known to be a counterfeit
Whoever uses a s genuine any stamp, knowing it to be counterfeit of any 
stamp issued by Government for the purpose of revenue, shall be punished 
with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven 
years, or with fine, or with both.
