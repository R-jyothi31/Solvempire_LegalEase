CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 1 year, or fine, or both—Non-cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the person whose 
trade or property mark is counterfeited with the permission of the court.
