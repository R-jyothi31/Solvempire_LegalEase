Illustration
A, being legally bound to produce a document before a 2[District Court], 
intentionally omits to produce the same. A has committed the offence defined 
in this section.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Para I
Punishment—Simple imprisonment for 1 month, or fine of 500 rupees, or both
—Non-cognizable—Bailable—Triable by the Court in which the offence is 
committed, subject to the provisions of Chapter XXVI or, if not committed in a
Court, any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
Para II
Punishment—Simple imprisonment for 6 months, or fine of 1,000 rupees, or 
both—Non-cognizable—Bailable—Triable by the Court in which the offence is 
committed, subject to the provisions of Chapter XXVI; or, if not committed in 
a Court, any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
