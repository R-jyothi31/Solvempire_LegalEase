Section 276. Sale of drug as a different drug or preparation
Whoever knowingly sells, or offers or exposes for sale, or issues from a 
dispensary for medicinal purposes, any drug or medical preparation, as a 
different drug or medical preparation, shall be punished with imprisonment of
either description for a term which may extend to six months, or with fine 
which may extend to one thousand rupees, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 6 months, or fine of 1,000 rupees, or both—
Non-cognizable—Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
