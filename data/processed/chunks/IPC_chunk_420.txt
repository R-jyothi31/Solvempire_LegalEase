Whoever throws or attempts to throw acid on any person or attempts to administer acid to any person, or 
attempts to use any other means, with the intention of causing permanent or partial damage or deformity or 
burns or maiming or disfigurement or disability or grievous hurt to that person, shall be punished with 
imprisonment of either description for a term which shall not be less than five years but which may extend to 
seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
