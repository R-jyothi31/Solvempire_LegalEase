CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 10 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—Non-
bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-compoundable.
Section 316. Causing death of quick unborn child by act amounting to culpable homicide
Whoever does any act under such circumstances, that if he thereby caused 
death he would be guilty of culpable homicide, and does by such act cause the 
death of a quick unborn child, shall be punished with imprisonment of either 
description for a term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable 
to fine.
