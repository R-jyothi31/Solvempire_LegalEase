Explanation I
For the purposes of this section, the word scurrilous shall be deemed to 
include any matter which is likely to be injurious to morality or is calculated to
injure any person:
Provided that it is not scurrilous to express in good faith anything whatever 
respecting the conduct of
(i) a public servant in the discharge of his public functions or respecting his 
character so far as his character appears in that conduct and no further; or
(ii) any person touching any public question, and respecting his character, so 
far as his character appears in that conduct and no further.
