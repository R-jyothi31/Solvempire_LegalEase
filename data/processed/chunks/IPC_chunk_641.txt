Section 498A vis-a-vis section 113 of Evidence Act
Section 498A of the Indian Penal Code or section 113A of the Indian Evidence 
Act has not introduced invidious classification qua the treatment of a married 
woman by her husband or relatives of her husband vis-a-vis the other 
offenders. On the other hand, such women form a class apart whom from 
those who are married more than seven years earlier to the commission of 
such offence, because, with the passage of time after marriage and birth of 
children, there are remote chances of treating a married woman with cruelty 
by her husband or his relatives. Thus, the classification is reasonable and has 
close nexus with the object sought to be achieved, i.e., eradication of the evil of
