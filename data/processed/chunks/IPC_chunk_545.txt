Section 417. Punishment for cheating
Whoever cheats shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for 
a term which may extend to one year, or with fine, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 1 year, or fine, or both—Non-cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the person cheated 
with the permission of the court.
