Section 153B. Imputations, assertions prejudicial to national-integration
1[153B. Imputations, assertions prejudicial to national-integration.— (1) 
Whoever, by words either spoken or written or by signs or by visible 
representations or otherwise, –
(a) Makes or publishes any imputation that any class of persons cannot, by 
reason or their being members of any religious, racial, language or regional 
group or caste or community, bear true faith and allegiance to Constitution of 
India as by law established or uphold the sovereignty and integrity of India, or
(b) Asserts, counsels, advises, propagates or publishes that any class or 
persons shall, by reason of their being members of any religious, racial, 
language or regional group or caste or community, be denied or deprived of
