CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Para I
Punishment—Imprisonment for 2 years and fine—Cognizable—Bailable—
Triable by any Magistrate.
Para II
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—
Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the person in possession of the 
house trespassed upon with the permission of the court.
