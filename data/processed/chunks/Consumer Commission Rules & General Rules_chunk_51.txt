(a) 
the appellant or his authorised agent fails to appear, the National Commission may, in its 
discretion, either dismiss the appeal or decide it ex-parte on the merits of the case;  
(b) 
the respondent or his authorised agent fails to appear, the National Commission shall 
proceed ex-parte and shall decide the appeal on the merits of the case.
