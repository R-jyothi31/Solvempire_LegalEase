Right of private defence to cause death
(i) Under what circumstances accused gave knife blow to the deceased could 
not be explained by accused, acquittal on ground of self defence not justified; 
State of Uttar Pradesh v. Laeeg, AIR 1999 SC 1942: 1999 (5) SCC 588.

incised wound, held exceeded the right of private defence, conviction under 
section 304 Part I proper; Suresh Singh v. State, AIR 1999 SC 1773: 1999 (2)
