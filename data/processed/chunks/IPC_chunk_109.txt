CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Para I
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years and fine—According as offence 
abetted is cognizable or non-cognizable—non-bailable—Triable by court by 
which offence abetted is triable—Non-compoundable.
Para II
Punishment—Imprisonment for 14 years and fine—According as offence 
abetted is cognizable or non-cognizable—non-bailable—Triable by court by 
which offence abetted is triable—Non-compoundable.
