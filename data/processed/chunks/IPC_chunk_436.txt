Section 344. Wrongful confinement for ten or more days
Whoever wrongfully confines any person for ten days, or more, shall be 
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which any extend
to three years, and shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 years and fine—Cognizable—Bailable—
Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the person confined with the 
permission of court.
Section 345. Wrongful confinement of person for whose liberation writ has been issued
Whoever keeps any person in wrongful confinement, knowing that a writ for 
the liberation of that person has been duly issued, shall be punished with 
imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two years 
in addition to any term of imprisonment to which he may be liable under any 
other section of this chapter.
