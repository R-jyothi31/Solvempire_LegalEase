CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Punishment of original sentence, or if part of the punishment 
has been undergone, the residue—Cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by the 
court by which the original offence was triable—Non-compoundable.
Section 228. Intentional insult or interruption to public servant sitting in judicial 
proceeding
Whoever intentionally offers any insult, or causes any interruption to any 
public servant, while such public servant is sitting in any stage of a judicial 
proceeding, shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term which 
may extend to six months, or with fine which may extend to one thousand 
rupees, or with both.
