Section 171G. False statement in connection with an election
1[171G. False statement in connection with an election.— Whoever with intent 
to affect the result of an election makes or publishes any statement purporting 
to be a statement of fact which is false and which he either knows or believes 
to be false or does not believe to be true, in relation to the personal character 
or conduct of any candidate shall be punished with fine.]
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Fine—Non-cognizable—Bailable—Triable by Magistrate of the 
first class—Non-compoundable.
