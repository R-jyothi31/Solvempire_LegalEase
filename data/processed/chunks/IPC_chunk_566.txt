CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 5 years and fine—Cognizable—Bailable—
Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
Section 441. Criminal trespass
Whoever enters into or upon property in the possession of another with intent 
to commit an offence or to intimidate, insult or annoy any person in 
possession of such property,
or having lawfully entered into or upon such property, unlawfully remains 
there with intent thereby to intimidate, insult or annoy any such person, or 
with intent to commit an offence, is said to commit “criminal trespass”.
