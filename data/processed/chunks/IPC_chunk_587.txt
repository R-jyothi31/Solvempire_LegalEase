Section 460. All persons jointly concerned in lurking house-trespass or house-breaking 
by night punishable where death or grievous hurt caused by one of them
If, at the time of the committing of lurking house-trespass by night or house-
breaking by night, any person guilty of such offence shall voluntarily cause or 
attempt to cause death or grievous hurt to any person, every person jointly 
concerned in committing such lurking house-trespass by night or house-
breaking by night, shall be punished with 1[imprisonment for life], or with
