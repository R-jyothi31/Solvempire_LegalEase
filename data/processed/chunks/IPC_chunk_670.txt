CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for life or imprisonment not exceeding half of the
longest term provided for the offence, or fine, or both—According as the 
offence is cognizable or non-cognizable—According as the offence attempted 
by the offender is bailable or not—Triable by the court by which the offence 
attempted is triable—Non-compoundable.
