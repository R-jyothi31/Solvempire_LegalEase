Illustration
A, knowing that B has committed dacoity, knowingly conceals B in order to 
screen him from legal punishment. Here, as B is liable to 1[imprisonment for 
life], A is liable to imprisonment of either description for a term not exceeding 
three years, and is also liable to fine.
