If act causing harm be done in consequence– and if any act for which 
the abettor is liable in consequence of the abetment, and which causes hurt to 
any person, is done, the abettor shall be liable to imprisonment of either 
description for a term which may extend to fourteen years, and shall also be 
liable to fine.
