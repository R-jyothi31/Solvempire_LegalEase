Section 493. Cohabitation caused by a man deceitfully inducing a belief of lawful 
marriage
Every man who by deceit causes any woman who is not lawfully married to 
him to believe that she is lawfully married to him and to cohabit or have sexual
intercourse with him in that belief, shall be punished with imprisonment of 
either description for a term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be 
liable to fine.
