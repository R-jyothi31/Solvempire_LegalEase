Section 186. Obstructing public servant in discharge of public functions
Whoever voluntarily obstructs any public servant in the discharge of his public
functions, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a 
term which may extend to three months, or with fine which may extend to five 
hundred rupees, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
