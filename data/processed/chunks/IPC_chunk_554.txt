Section 426. Punished for mischief
Whoever commits mischief shall be punished with imprisonment of either 
description for a term which may extend to three months, or with fine, or with 
both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 months, or fine, or both—Non-cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the person to whom 
the loss or damage is caused.
