Seventh Exception.—Censure passed in good faith by person having 
lawful authority over another.—It is not defamation in a person having 
over another any authority, either conferred by law or arising out of a lawful 
contract made with that other, to pass in good faith any censure on the 
conduct of that other in matters to which such lawful authority relates.
