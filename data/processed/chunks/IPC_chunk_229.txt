CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Para I
Punishment—Imprisonment for life, or rigorous imprisonment for 10 years 
and fine—Non-cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-
compoundable.
Para II
Punishment—Death or as above—Non-cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by 
Court of Session—Non-compoundable.
