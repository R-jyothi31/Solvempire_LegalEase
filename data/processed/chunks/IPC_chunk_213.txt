Section 184. Obstructing sale of property offered for sale by authority of public servant
Whoever intentionally obstructs any sale of property offered for sale by the 
lawful authority of any public servant as such, shall be punished with 
imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to one 
month, or with fine which may extend to five hundred rupees, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 1 month or fine of 500 rupees, or both—Non-
cognizable—Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
