Illustrations
(a) A sits dharna at Z’s door with the intention of causing it to be believed that,
by so sitting, he renders Z an object of Divine displeasure. A has committed 
the offence defined in this section.
(b) A threatens Z that, unless Z performs a certain act, A will kill one of A’s 
own children, under such circumstances that the killing would be believed to 
render Z an object of Divine displeasure. A has committed the offence defined 
in this section.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 1 year, or fine, or both—Non-cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the person against 
whom the offence was committed.
