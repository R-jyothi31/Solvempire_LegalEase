CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Para I
Punishment—Same as for abetment of the offence which is the object of the 
conspiracy—According as the offence which is the object of conspiracy is 
cognizable or non-cognizable—According as offence which is object of 
conspiracy is bailable or non-bailable—Triable by court by which abetment of 
the offence which is the object of conspiracy is triable—Non-compoundable.
Para II
Punishment—Imprisonment for six months or fine, or both—non-cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
