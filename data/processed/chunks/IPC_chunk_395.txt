Section 302. Punishment for murder
Whoever commits murder shall be punished with death, or 1[imprisonment for
life] and shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Death, or imprisonment for life, and fine—Cognizable—Non-
bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-compoundable.
1. Subs. by Act 26 of 1955, sec. 117 and Sch., for “transportation for life” (w.e.f. 
1-1-1956).
Section 303. Punishment for murder by life-convict
Whoever, being under sentence of 1[imprisonment for life], commits murder, 
shall be punished with death.
