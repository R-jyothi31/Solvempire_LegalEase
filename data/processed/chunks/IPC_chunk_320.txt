Whoever, fraudulently or with intent to cause loss to the Government, 
removes or effaces from any substance, bearing any stamp issued by 
Government for the purpose of revenue, any writing or document for which 
such stamp has been used, or removes from any writing or document a stamp 
which has been used for such writing or document, in order that such stamp 
may be used for a different writing or document, shall be punished with 
imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three 
years, or with fine, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
