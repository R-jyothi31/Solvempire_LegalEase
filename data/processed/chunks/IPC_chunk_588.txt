Section 461. Dishonestly breaking open receptacle containing property
Whoever dishonestly or with intent to commit mischief, breaks open or 
unfastens any closed receptacle which contains or which be believes to contain
property, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term
which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 2 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
