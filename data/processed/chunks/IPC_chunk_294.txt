Section 232. Counterfeiting Indian coin
Whoever counterfeits, or knowingly performs any part of the process of 
counterfeiting 1[Indian coin], shall be punished with 2[imprisonment for life], 
or with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extent to ten 
years, and shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for life, or imprisonment for 10 years and fine—
Cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-compoundable.

1. Subs. by the A.O. 1950, for “the Queen’s coin”.
