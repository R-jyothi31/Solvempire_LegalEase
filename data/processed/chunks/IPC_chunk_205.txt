Section 178. Refusing oath or affirmation when duly required by public servant to make 
it
Whoever refuses to bind himself by an oath 1[or affirmation] to state the truth,
when required so to bind himself by a public servant legally competent to 
require that he shall so bind himself, shall be punished with simple 
imprisonment for a term which may extend to six months, or with fine which 
may extend to one thousand rupees, or with both.
