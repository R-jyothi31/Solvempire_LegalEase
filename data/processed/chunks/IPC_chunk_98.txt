Section 108A. Abetment in India of offences outside India
1108A. Abetment in India of offences outside India.- A person abets an offence 
within the meaning of this Code who, in 2[India], abets the commission of any 
act without and beyond 2[India] which would constitute an offence if 
committed in 2[India].
Illustration
A, in 2[India], instigates B, a foreigner in Goa, to commit a murder in Goa.
A is guilty of abetting murder.
