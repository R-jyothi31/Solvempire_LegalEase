Section 283. Danger or obstruction in public way or line of navigation
Whoever, by doing any act, or by omitting to take order with any property in 
his possession or under his charge, causes danger, obstruction or injury to any
person in any public way or public line of navigation, shall be punished with 
fine which may extend to two hundred rupees.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Fine of 200 rupees—Cognizable—Bailable—Triable by any 
Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
