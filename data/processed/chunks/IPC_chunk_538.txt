Section 412. Dishonestly receiving property stolen in the commission of a dacoity
Whoever dishonestly receives or retains any stolen property, the possession 
whereof he knows or has reason to believe to have been transferred by the 
commission of dacoity, or dishonestly receives from a person, whom he knows
or has reason to believe to belong or to have belonged to a gang of dacoity, 
property which he knows or has reason to believe to have been stolen, shall be 
punished with 1[imprisonment for life], or with rigorous imprisonment for a 
term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.
