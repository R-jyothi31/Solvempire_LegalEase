CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 6 months, or fine of 1,000 rupees, or both—
Cognizable—Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
Section 285. Negligent conduct with respect to fire or combustible matter
Whoever does, with fire or any combustible matter, any act so rashly or 
negligently as to endanger human life, or to be likely to cause hurt or injury to 
any other person,
or knowingly or negligently omits to take such order with any fire or any
