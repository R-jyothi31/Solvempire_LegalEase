Section 244. Person employed in mint causing coin to be of different weight or 
composition from that fixed by law
Whoever, being employed in any mint lawfully established in 1[India], does 
any act, or omits what he is legally bound to do, with the intention of causing 
any coin issued from that mint to be of a different weight or composition from 
the weight or composition fixed by law, shall be punished with imprisonment
