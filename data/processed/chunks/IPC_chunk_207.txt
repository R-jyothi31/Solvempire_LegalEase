CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Simple imprisonment for 6 months, or fine of 1,000 rupees, or 
both—Non-cognizable—Bailable—Triable by the court in which the offence is 
committed, subject to the provisions of Chapter XXVI; or, if not committed in 
a court, any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
Section 180. Refusing to sign statement
Whoever refuses to sign any statement made by him, when required to sign 
that statement by a public servant legally competent to require that he shall 
sign that statement, shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term 
which may extend to three months, or with fine which may extend to five 
hundred rupees, or with both.
