Section 366A. Procreation of minor girl
1[366A. procreation of minor girl.—Whoever, by any means whatsoever, 
induces any minor girl under the age of eighteen years to go from any place or 
to do any act with intent that such girl may be, or knowing that it is likely that 
she will be, forced or seduced to illicit intercourse with another person shall be
punishable with imprisonment which may extend to ten years, and shall also 
be liable to fine.]
