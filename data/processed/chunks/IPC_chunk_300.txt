Section 239. Delivery of coin, possessed with knowledge that it is counterfeit
Whoever, having any counterfeit coin, which at the time when he became 
possessed of it knew to be counterfeit, fraudulently or with intent that fraud 
may be committed, delivers the same to any person, or attempts to induce any 
person to receive it shall be punished with imprisonment of either description 
for a term which may extend to five years, and shall also be liable to fine.
