CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Simple imprisonment for 3 months, or fine of 500 rupees, or 
both—Non-cognizable—Bailable—Triable by the court in which the offence is 
committed, subject to the provisions of Chapter XXVI; or, if not committed in 
a court, any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
Section 181. False statement on oath or affirmation to public servant or person 
authorised to administer an oath or affirmation
Whoever, being legally bound by an oath 1[or affirmation] to state the truth on 
any subject to any public servant or other person authorized by law to 
administer such oath 2[or affirmation], makes, to such public servant or other 
person as aforesaid, touching the subject, any statement which is false, and 
which he either knows or believes to be false or does not believe to be true, 
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which 
may extend to three years, and shall also be liable to fine.
