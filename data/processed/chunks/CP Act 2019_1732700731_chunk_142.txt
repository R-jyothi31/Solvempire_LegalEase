99. (1) Without prejudice to the foregoing provisions of this Act, the Central Authority,
shall, in exercise of its powers or the performance of its functions under this Act, be bound
by such directions on questions of policy, as the Central Government may give in writing to
it from time to time:
Provided that the Central Authority shall, as far as practicable, be given an opportunity
to express its views before any direction is given under this sub-section.
(2) The decision of the Central Government whether a question is one of policy or not
shall be final.
100. The provisions of this Act shall be in addition to and not in derogation of the
provisions of any other law for the time being in force.
