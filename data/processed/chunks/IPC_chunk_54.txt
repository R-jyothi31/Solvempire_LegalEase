Section 74. Limit of solitary confinement
In executing a sentence of solitary confinement, such confinement shall in no 
case exceed fourteen days at a time, with intervals between the periods of 
solitary confinement of not less duration than such periods: and when the 
imprisonment awarded shall exceed three months, the solitary confinement 
shall not exceed seven days in any one month of the whole imprisonment 
awarded, with intervals between the periods of solitary confinement of not less
duration than such periods.
