CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 years, or fine, or both—Non-cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
Section 206. Fraudulent removal or concealment of property to prevent its seizure as 
forfeited or in execution
Whoever fraudulently removes, conceals, transfers or delivers to any person 
any property or any interest therein, intending thereby to prevent that 
property or interest therein from being taken as a forfeiture or in satisfaction 
of a fine, under a sentence which has been pronounced, or which he knows to 
be likely to be pronounced, by a Court of Justice or other competent authority,
or from being taken in execution of a decree or order which has been made, or 
which he knows to be likely to be made by a Court of Justice in a civil suit,
