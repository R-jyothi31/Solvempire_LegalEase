CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 2 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
Section 225. Resistance or obstruction to lawful apprehension of another person
Whoever intentionally offers any resistance or illegal obstruction to the lawful 
apprehension of any other person for an offence, or rescues or attempts to 
rescue any other person from any custody in which that person is lawfully 
detained for an offence, shall be punished with imprisonment of either 
description for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with 
both;
