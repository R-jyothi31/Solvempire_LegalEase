Section 366B. Importation of girl from foreign country
1[366B. Importation of girl from foreign country.—Whoever imports 
into 2[India] from any country outside India 3[or from the State of Jammu and 
Kashmir] any girl under the age of twenty-one years with intent that she may 
be, or knowing it to be likely that she will be, forced or seduced to illicit 
intercourse with another person, 4[***] shall be punishable with imprisonment
which may extend to ten years and shall also be liable to fine.]
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 10 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable
—Triable by Court of Session—Non-compoundable.
