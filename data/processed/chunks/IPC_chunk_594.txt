(f) Z’s will contains the these words—“I direct that all my remaining property 
be equally divided between A, B and C.” A dishonestly scratches out B’s name, 
intending that it may be believed that the whole was left to himself and C. A 
has committed forgery.
(g) A endorses a Government promissory note and makes it payable to Z or his
order by writing on the bill the words “Pay to Z or his order” and signing the 
endorsement. B dishonestly erases the words “Pay to Z or his order”, and 
thereby converts the special endorsement into a blank endorsement. B 
commits forgery.
