CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 2 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
State Amendments
Andhra Pradesh
For section 354, the following section shall be substituted, namely
354. Assault or criminal force to woman with intent to outrage her modesty.—
Whoever assaults or uses criminal force to any woman intending to outrage or 
knowing it to be likely that he will thereby outrage her modesty, shall be 
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which shall not 
be less than five years but which may extend to seven years and shall also be 
liable to fine:
