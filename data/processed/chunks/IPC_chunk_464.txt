Section 365. Kidnapping or abducting with intent secretly and wrongfully to confine 
person
Whoever kidnaps or abducts any person with intent to cause that person to be 
secretly and wrongfully confined, shall be punished with imprisonment of 
either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also 
be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—
Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
