Illustrations
(a) A has a letter of credit upon B for rupees 10,000 written by Z. A, in order to
defraud B, adds a cipher to the 10,000, and makes the sum 1,00,000 
intending that it may be believed by B that Z so wrote the letter. A has 
committed forgery.
(b) A, without Z’s authority, affixes Z’s seal to a document purporting to be a 
conveyance of an estate from Z to A, with the intention of selling the estate to 
B, and thereby of obtaining from B the purchase-money. A has committed 
forgery.
