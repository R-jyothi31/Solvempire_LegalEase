Section 289. Negligent conduct with respect to animal
Whoever knowingly or negligently omits to take such order with any animal in
his possession as is sufficient to guard against any probable danger to human 
life, or any probable danger of grievous hurt from such animal, shall be 
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may 
extend to six months, or with fine which may extend to one thousand rupees, 
or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 6 months, or fine of 1,000 rupees, or both—
Cognizable—Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
