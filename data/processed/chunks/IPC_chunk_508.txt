Whoever commits extortion by putting any person in fear of an accusation 
against that person or any other, of having committed or attempted to commit
any offence punishable with death, or with 1[imprisonment for life], or with 
imprisonment for a term which may extend to ten years or of having 
attempted to induce any other person to commit such offence, shall be 
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may 
extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine; and, if the offence be one 
punishable under section 377 of this Code, may be punished with 1[imprison-
ment for life].
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 10 years and fine—Cognizable—Bailable—
Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
1. Subs. by Act 26 of 1955, sec. 117 and Sch., for “transportation for life” (w.e.f. 
1-1-1956).
