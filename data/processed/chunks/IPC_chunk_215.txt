Section 187. Omission to assist public servant when bound by law to give assistance
Whoever, being bound by law to render or furnish assistance to any public 
servant in the execution of his public duty, intentionally omits to give such 
assistance, shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term which may 
extend to one month, or with fine which may extend to two hundred rupees, or
