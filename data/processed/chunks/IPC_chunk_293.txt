Section 231. Counterfeiting coin
Whoever counterfeits or knowingly performs any part of the process of 
counterfeiting coin, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description
for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
Explanation
A person commits this offence who intending to practice deception, or 
knowing it to be likely that deception will thereby be practiced, causes a 
genuine coin to appear like a different coin.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—
Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
