Section 247. Fraudulently or dishonestly diminishing weight or altering composition of 
Indian coin
Whoever fraudulently or dishonestly performs on 1[any Indian coin] any 
operation which diminishes the weight or alters the composition of that coin, 
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which 
may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—
Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
1. Subs. by the A.O. 1950, for “any of the Queen’s coin”.
