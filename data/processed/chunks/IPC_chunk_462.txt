Illustrations
(a) A kidnaps Z from 2[India], intending or knowing it to be likely that Z may 
be sacrificed to an idol. A has committed the offence defined in this section.
(b) A forcibly carries or entices B away from his home in order that B may be 
murdered. A has committed the offence defined in this section..
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for life, or rigorous imprisonment for 10 years 
and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-
compoundable.
