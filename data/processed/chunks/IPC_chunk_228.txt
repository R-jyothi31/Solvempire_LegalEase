Section 194. Giving or fabricating false evidence with intent to procure conviction of 
capital offence
Whoever gives or fabricates false evidence, intending thereby to cause, or 
knowing it to be likely that he will thereby cause, any person to be convicted of
an offence which is capital 1[by the law for the time being in force in 2[India]] 
shall be punished with 3[imprisonment for life], or with rigorous 
imprisonment for a term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be 
liable to fine;
