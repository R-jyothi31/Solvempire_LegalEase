(3 ) Where the offence involves the trafficking of more than one person, it shall
be punishable with rigorous imprisonment for a term which shall not be less 
than ten years but which may extend to imprisonment for life, and shall also 
be liable to fine.
(4 ) Where the offence involves the trafficking of a minor, it shall be 
punishable with rigorous imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than
ten years, but which may extend to imprisonment for life, and shall also be 
liable to fine.
(5 ) Where the offence involves the trafficking of more than one minor, it shall 
be punishable with rigorous imprisonment for a term which shall not be less 
than fourteen years, but which may extend to imprisonment for life, and shall 
also be liable to fine.
