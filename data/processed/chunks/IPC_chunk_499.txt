Section 379. Punishment for theft
Whoever commits theft shall be punished with imprisonment of either 
description for a term which may extend to three years, or with fine, or with 
both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—Non-
bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the owner of the 
property stolen with the permission of the court.
