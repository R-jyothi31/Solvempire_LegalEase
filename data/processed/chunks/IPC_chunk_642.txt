Unhappiness between husband and wife
Where the prosecution relied only on incident of unhappiness of deceased 
with her husband and the allegation was only in form of suggestion, it does not
establish criminal offence under either or both of the charges, hence 
conviction under section 498A is improper; State v. K. Sridhar, 2000 Cr LJ 
328 (Kant).
