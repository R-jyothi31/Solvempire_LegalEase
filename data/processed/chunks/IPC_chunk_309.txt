Section 249. Altering appearance of Indian coin with intent that it shall pass as coin of 
different description
whoever performs on 1[any Indian coin] any operation which alters the 
appearance of that coin, with the intention that the said coin shall pass as a 
coin of a different description, shall be punished with imprisonment of either 
description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be 
liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 7 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—
Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
