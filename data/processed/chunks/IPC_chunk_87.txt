Maharashtra
In section 103, add the following at the end, namely:
“Fifthly.—Mischief by fire or any explosive substance committed on any 
property used or intended to be used for the purposes of Government or any 
local authority, statutory body, company owned or controlled by Government, 
railway or tramway, or on any vehicle used or adapted to be used, for the 
carriage of passengers for hire or reward”.
[Vide Maharashtra Act 19 of 1971, sec. 26 (w.e.f. 31-12-1971)].
