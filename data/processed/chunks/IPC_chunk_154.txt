Section 145. Joining or continuing in unlawful assembly, knowing it has been 
commanded to disperse
Whoever joins or continues in an unlawful assembly, knowing that such 
unlawful assembly has been commanded in the manner prescribed by law to 
disperse, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term
which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 2 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
