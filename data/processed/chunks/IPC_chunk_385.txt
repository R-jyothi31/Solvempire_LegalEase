Injuries on vital and non-vital parts of body of the deceased
Infliction of the injury on the vital part of the body with the agricultural 
instrument by the enraged accused in a sudden quarrel—Held, accused did not
cause the injury intentionally; Patel Rasiklal Becharbhai v. State of Gujarat, 
AIR 1992 SC 1150.
