Section 132. Abetment of mutiny, if mutiny is committed in consequence thereof
Whoever abets the committing of mutiny by an officer, soldier, 1[sailor or 
airman] in the Army, 2[Navy or Air Force] of the 3[Government of India], shall,
if mutiny be committed in consequence of that abetment, be punished with 
death or with 4[imprisonment for life], or imprisonment of either description 
for a term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Death, or imprisonment for life, or imprisonment for 10 years 
and fine-Cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-
compoundable.
1. Subs. by Act 10 of 1927, sec. 2 and Sch. I, for “or sailor”.
