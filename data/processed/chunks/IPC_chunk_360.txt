CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—On first conviction, with imprisonment for 3 years, and with fine
of 2,000 rupees, and, in the event of second of subsequent conviction, with 
imprisonment for 7 years, and with fine of 5,000 rupees—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
