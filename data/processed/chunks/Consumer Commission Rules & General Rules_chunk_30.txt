2. 
Definitions. —(1) ln these rules, unless the context otherwise requires,— 
 
(a) 
'Act' means the Consumer Protection Act 2019 (35 of 2019); 
(b) 
'appellant' means a person who makes an appeal against the order of the National 
Commission, the State Commission or the District Commission, as the case may 
be; 
(c) 
'authorised agent' means a person duly authorised by a party to present any 
complaint, appeal or reply on behalf of such party before the National 
Commission, the State Commission, or the District Commission, as the case may 
be; 
(d) 
‘Commission’ means the District Commission, the State Commission, or the 
National Commission, as the case may be; 
(e) 
‘Consumer Welfare Fund’ means the Consumer Welfare Fund established by a 
State Government or, as the case may be, by the  Central Government under 
section 57 of the Central Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017 (12 of 2017) ;
