90. (1) Whoever, by himself or by any other person on his behalf, manufactures for
sale or stores or sells or distributes or imports any product containing an adulterant shall be
punished, if such act—
(a) does not result in any injury to the consumer, with imprisonment for a term
which may extend to six months and with fine which may extend to one lakh rupees;
(b) causing injury not amounting to grievous hurt to the consumer, with
imprisonment for a term which may extend to one year and with fine which may extend
to three lakh rupees;
(c) causing injury resulting in grievous hurt to the consumer, with imprisonment
for a term which may extend to seven years and with fine which may extend to five lakh
rupees; and
