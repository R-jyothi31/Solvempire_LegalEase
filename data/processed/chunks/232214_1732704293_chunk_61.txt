9.   Application of e-commerce rules.–– The direct sellers as well as the direct selling entities using  
e-commerce platforms for sale shall comply with the requirements of the Consumer Protection (e-
Commerce) Rules, 2020. 
10.    Prohibition of Pyramid Scheme and money circulation scheme.––No direct selling entity or direct 
seller shall–– 
 (a) promote a Pyramid Scheme or enroll any person to such scheme or participate in such arrangement in 
any manner whatsoever in the garb of doing direct selling business; 
(b) participate in money circulation scheme in the garb of doing direct selling business.
