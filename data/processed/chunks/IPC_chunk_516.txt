Section 396. Dacoity with murder
If any one of five or more persons, who are conjointly committing dacoity, 
commits murder in so committing dacoity, every one of those persons shall be 
punished with death, or 1[imprisonment for life], or rigorous imprisonment for
term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Death, imprisonment for life, or rigorous imprisonment for 10 
years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-
compoundable.
