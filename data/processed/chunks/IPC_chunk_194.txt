Section 174. Non-attendance in obedience to an order form public servant
Whoever, being legally bound to attend in person or by an agent at a certain 
place and time in obedience to a summons, notice, order or proclamation 
proceeding from any public servant legally competent, as such public servant, 
to issue the same,
intentionally omits to attend at that place or time, or departs from the place 
where he is bound to attend before the time at which it is lawful for him to 
depart,
shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term which may extend to 
one month, or with fine which may extend to five hundred rupees, or with 
both,
