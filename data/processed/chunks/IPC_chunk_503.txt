Illustrations
(a) A commits theft on property in Z’s possession; and, while committing this 
theft, he has a loaded pistol under his garment, having provided this pistol for 
the purpose of hurting Z in case Z should resist. A has committed the offence 
defined in this section.
(b) A picks Z’s pocket, having posted several of his companions near him, in 
order that they may restrain Z, if Z should perceive what is passing and should
resist, or should attempt to apprehend A. A has committed the offence defined
in this section
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Rigorous imprisonment for 10 years and fine—Cognizable—
Non-bailable—Triable by Magistrate of the first class—Non-compoundable.
