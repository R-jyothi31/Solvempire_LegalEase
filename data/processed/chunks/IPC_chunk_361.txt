Section 294. Obscene acts and songs
1[294. Obscene acts and songs.—Whoever, to the annoyance of others
(a) does any obscene act in any public place, or
(b) sings, recites or utters any obscene song, ballad or words, in or near any 
public place,
shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which 
may extend to three months, or with fine, or with both.]
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 months, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
