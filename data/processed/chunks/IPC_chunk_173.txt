Whoever is engaged, or hired, or offers or attempts to be hired or engaged, to 
do or assist in doing any of the acts specified in Section 141, shall be punished 
with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to six 
months, or with fine, or with both,
or to go armed.— and whoever, being so engaged or hired as aforesaid, goes 
armed, or engages or offers to go armed, with any deadly weapon or with 
anything which used as a weapon of offence is likely to cause death, shall be 
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may 
extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Para I
Punishment—Imprisonment for 6 months, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
Para II
Punishment—Imprisonment for 2 years, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
