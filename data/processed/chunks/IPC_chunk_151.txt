Section 141. Unlawful assembly
An assembly of five or more persons is designated an “unlawful assembly”, if 
the common object of the persons composing that assembly is
First.— To overawe by criminal force, or show of criminal force, 1[the Central 
or any State Government or Parliament or the Legislature of any State], or any
public servant in the exercise of the lawful power of such public servant; or
Second.— To resist the execution of any law, or of any legal process; or
