Section 450. House-trespass in order to commit offence punishable with imprisonment 
for life
Whoever commits house-trespass in order to the committing of any offence 
punishable with 1[imprisonment for life], shall be punished with 
imprisonment of either description for a term not exceeding ten years, and 
shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 10 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable
—Triable by Court of Session—Non-compoundable.
