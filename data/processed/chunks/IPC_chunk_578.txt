Section 453. Punishment for lurking house-trespass or house-breaking
Whoever commits lurking house-trespass or house-breaking, shall be 
punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may 
extend to two years, and shall also be liable to fine.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 2 years and fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—
Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
