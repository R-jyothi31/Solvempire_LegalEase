CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 1 month, or fine of 500 rupees, or both—Non-
cognizable—Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Compoundable by the 
person to whom the hurt is caused.
Section 335. Voluntarily causing grievous hurt on provocation
Whoever 1[voluntarily] causes grievous hurt on grave and sudden provocation,
if he neither intends nor knows himself to be likely to cause grievous hurt to 
any person other than the person who gave the provocation, shall be punished 
with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to four 
years or with fine which may extend to two thousand rupees, or with both
