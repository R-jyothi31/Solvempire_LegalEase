Section 150. Hiring, or conniving at hiring, of persons to join unlawful assembly
Whoever hires or engages, or employs, or promotes, or connives at the hiring, 
engagement or employment of any person to join or become a member of any 
unlawful assembly, shall be punishable as a member of such unlawful 
assembly, and for any offence which may be committed by any such person as 
a member of such unlawful assembly in pursuance of such hiring, engagement
or employment, in the same manner as if he had been a member of such 
unlawful assembly, or himself had committed such offence.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—The same as for a member of such assembly, and for any offence
committed by any members of such assembly—Cognizable—According as 
offence is bailable or non-bailable—Triable by court by which the offence is 
triable—Non-compoundable.
