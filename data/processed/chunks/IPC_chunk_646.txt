Second Exception.—Public conduct of public servants.—It is not 
defamation to express in a good faith any opinion whatever respecting the 
conduct of a public servant in the discharge of his public functions, or 
respecting his character, so far as his character appears in that conduct, and 
no further.
Third Exception.—Conduct of any person touching any public 
question.—It is not defamation to express in good faith any opinion whatever 
respecting the conduct of any person touching any public question, and 
respecting his character, so far as his character appears in that conduct, and 
no further.
