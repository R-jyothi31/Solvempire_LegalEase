Punishment—Imprisonment for 3 years and fine—Non-cognizable—Bailable—

Section 485. Making or possession of any instrument for counterfeiting a property mark
1[485. Making or possession of any instrument for counterfeiting a property 
mark.—Whoever makes or has in his possession any die, plate or other 
instrument for the purpose of counterfeiting a property mark, or has in his 
possession a property mark for the purpose of denoting that any goods belong 
to a person to whom they do not belong, shall be punished with imprisonment 
of either description for a term which may extend to three years or with fine, 
or with both.]
