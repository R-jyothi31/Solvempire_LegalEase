Section 151. Knowingly joining or continuing in assembly of five or more persons after it 
has been commanded to disperse
Whoever knowingly joins or continues in any assembly of five or more persons
likely to cause a disturbance of the public peace, after such assembly has been 
lawfully commanded to disperse, shall be punished with imprisonment of 
either description for a term which may extend to six months, or with fine, or 
with both.
Explanation
If the assembly is an unlawful assembly with the meaning of section 141, the 
offender will be punishable under section 145.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 6 months, or fine, or both—Cognizable—
Bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
