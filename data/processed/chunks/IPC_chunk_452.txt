Section 355. Assault or criminal force with intent to dishonour person, otherwise than on
grave provocation
Whoever assaults or uses criminal force to any person, intending thereby to 
dishonour that person, otherwise than on grave and sudden provocation given
by that person, shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may 
extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
