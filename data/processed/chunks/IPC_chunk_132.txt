Explanation 1
The expression “disaffection” includes disloyalty and all feelings of enmity.
Explanation 2
Comments expressing disapprobation of the measures of the Government with
a view to obtain their alteration by lawful means, without exciting or 
attempting to excite hatred, contempt or disaffection, do not constitute an 
offence under this section.
Explanation 3
Comments expressing disapprobation of the administrative or other action of 
the Government without exciting or attempting to excite hatred, contempt or 
disaffection, do not constitute an offence under this section.
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for life and fine, or imprisonment for 3 years and
fine, or fine—Cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by Court of Session—Non-
compoundable.
