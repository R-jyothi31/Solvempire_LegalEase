Right to private defence
(i) The accused is not required to prove the plea of private defence of person 
beyond reasonable manner of doubt. The onus on the accused is only to show 
that the defence version is probable one which is reflected from the salient 
features and the circumstances in the prosecution case itself; Sawai Ram v. 
State of Rajasthan, (1997) 2 Crimes 148 (Raj).
