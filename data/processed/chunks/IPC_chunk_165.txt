Explanation
”Arms” means articles of any description designed or adapted as weapons for 
offence or defence and includes fire-arms, sharp edged weapons, lathis, 
dandas and sticks.]
CLASSIFICATION OF OFFENCE
Punishment—Imprisonment for 6 months and fine of 2000 rupees—
Cognizable—Non-bailable—Triable by any Magistrate—Non-compoundable.
